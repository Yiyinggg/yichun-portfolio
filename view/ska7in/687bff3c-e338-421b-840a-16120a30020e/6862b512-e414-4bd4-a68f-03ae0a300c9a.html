YICHUN JIN

STYLIST AND CREATIVE DIRECTOR WORKING ACROSS FASHION IMAGE-MAKING, EDITORIAL STYLING AND VISUAL STORYTELLING. YICHUN’S PRACTICE EXPLORES THE RELATIONSHIP BETWEEN CLOTHING, BODY, IDENTITY AND ATMOSPHERE, CREATING IMAGES THAT SIT BETWEEN REALITY AND IMAGINATION.

WITH AN INTEREST IN DREAMS, MEMORY, FEMININITY AND EMOTIONAL SPACES, HER WORK OFTEN COMBINES SOFT SURREALISM WITH A STRONG SENSE OF CHARACTER AND MOOD. THROUGH STYLING, COLOUR, TEXTURE AND SET DESIGN, SHE BUILDS VISUAL NARRATIVES THAT FEEL INTIMATE, POETIC AND SLIGHTLY UNSETTLING.

YICHUN WORKS BETWEEN LONDON AND CHINA.
